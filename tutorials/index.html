So, you want to make a bot

If you've never made a bot before, but have some programming experience, then you should head over to Part 1 - Ideas.

If you've also never had any interaction with programming, then you can still make a bot.

However, keep in mind that you will be starting from the bottom. This doesn't mean that your dreams of being a botmin are impossible; it just means that you will have to put in more effort.

If you feel confident that you can learn quickly, and manage the knowledge gaps you might come across, then proceed to Part 1 - Ideas.

If you would prefer to come back to make a bot after working on some simpler projects: feel free to hit up the Discord for some advice on where to learn.
